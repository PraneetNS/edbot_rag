Certifications and Credentials
------------------------------
Edutainer offers verified, industry-aligned credentials and certificates. In collaboration with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), we provide learners with certificates that validate their practical skills:
1. Course Completion Certificate: Awarded to students who finish all course modules and meet the passing criteria.
2. Virtual Internship Certificate: Jointly issued by Edutainer and VTU, validating hands-on training and internship hours (typically 4 to 8 weeks).
3. Professional Specialization Badge: Issued upon mastering a series of courses in a learning path.

Requirements to Earn a Certificate:
- Complete 100% of the online course video lectures and learning materials.
- Score 70% or higher on all modular quizzes and tests.
- Submit the final capstone project or internship assignment and obtain a passing grade from the mentors.

Certificate Verification:
Every certificate includes a unique Certificate ID (e.g., CERT-VTU-XXXXX) and a QR code linking to Edutainer's online verification portal (https://beta.edutainer.in/verify) for validation by employers.


Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
--------------------------------
Q: How do I access course modules?
A: Login to Edutainer LMS (eLMS) and open your enrolled course dashboard.

Q: What are the office support hours?
A: Phone support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM. You can call +91 9901582101.

Q: Who do I email for support?
A: You can email info@edutainer.in or support@edutainer.in for any course, platform, or account inquiries.

Q: Where is the Edutainer office located?
A: The main office is located at No - 110, 7th Cross Rd, Dollar Layout, BTM 2nd Stage, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560076.

Q: How do I register an account on Edutainer?
A: Visit the Edutainer website homepage (https://beta.edutainer.in), click the "Register" button in the navigation header, fill in your details, and verify your email.

Q: What should I do if my course video is not loading?
A: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try using an incognito window. If the issue persists, contact support@edutainer.in.

Q: How do I download my certificate?
A: Once you complete all requirements for your course or virtual internship, go to your LMS Dashboard, open your enrolled program, and click on the "Download Certificate" button.

Q: Are Edutainer certificates recognized by employers?
A: Yes. Because they are backed by Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and include hands-on project verification, employers recognize them as evidence of practical skills.

Q: Can I share my certificate on LinkedIn?
A: Yes, you can add your certificate directly to your LinkedIn profile under "Licenses & Certifications" using the unique verification URL.

Q: Is there an extra charge for certificates?
A: No. Edutainer certificates are included for free upon completing any registered course or virtual internship program.
